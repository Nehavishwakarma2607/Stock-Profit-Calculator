Profit

The amount of money you made from the trade.
Profit=(Selling Price*No of shares - Selling Commission)-(Buying Price*No of shares + Buying Commission)

ROI

The percentage of profit you made from the trade.
ROI=(Profit/(Buying Price*No of shares + Buying Commission))*100

Break Even Price

The price at which you would break even on the trade.
Break Even Price=(Buy Price* No Of Shares + Buy Commission + Sell Commission)/No Of Shares
